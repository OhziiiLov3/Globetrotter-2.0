Food Guide

Trattoria Da Nennella

Address: Vico Lungo Teatro Nuovo, Naples

Rustic Neapolitan favorites in a bustling, family-style atmosphere with loud, passionate energy.

Osteria dei Vespri

Address: Piazza Croce dei Vespri, Palermo

Modern Sicilian dishes made with local ingredients served in a refined, minimalist setting.

Ristorante Alfonso 1890

Address: Corso Sant'Agata, Sorrento

A Michelin-starred experience fusing tradition with innovation, nestled on the Sorrentine Peninsula.

Antica Trattoria da Tito

Address: Via San Gallo, Florence

Homestyle southern dishes and warm service in a rustic trattoria dating back over a century.
